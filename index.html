Lectionary Readings Parser

This app takes readings like this:

1You are to be praised, O | God, in Zion;
  to you shall vows | be fulfilled.
2To you, the one | who answers prayer,
  to you all | flesh shall come.
3Our sins are stronger | than we are,
  but you blot out | our transgressions.

And converts them into this:

L: You are to be praised, O God, in Zion; to you shall vows be fulfilled.
C: To you, the one who answers prayer, to you all flesh shall come.
L: Our sins are stronger than we are, but you blot out our transgressions.


Instructions:

It is currently configured to adapt Sunday lectionaries only from sundaysandseasons.com. You must be a subscriber in order to use Sundays and Seasons. To quickly extract lectionary readings and convert them, follow the instructions below.

  1. Sign up or sign in to Sundays and Seasons
  2. Click "Day Texts"
  3. Click "Read in Full"
  4. Select all text on the page (ctrl-A)
  5. Copy text (ctrl-C)
  6. Paste text into input field (ctrl-V)
